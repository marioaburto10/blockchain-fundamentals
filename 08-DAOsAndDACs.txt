Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)

- Also called Decentralized Autonomous Corporations but DAO is more commonly used because not all organizations are corporations

- Is a collection of smart contracts

- Reaching multiple agreements and they are all self-executing and happening automatically on a Blockchain

- Can have multiple combinations of different parties exchanging value and reaching agreements due to the distributed networks on Blockchain

- Can be used with artificial intelligence since everything is automated
