WHAT IS A BLOCKCHAIN?

- blockchain can provide a single source of truth that is verifiable, tamperproof, and unchageable.

- A Blockchain is a constantly growing ledger that keeps a permanent record of all the transactions that have taken place, in a secure, chronological and immutable way. 
	- it is a ledger (a file in which business transactions are recorded)
	- constantly growing
	- keeps track of all transactions
	- permanent
	- secure
	- chronological
	- Immutable (As transactions become linked together, they can never be changed)
